The Story Behind Oui PAWD

Built out of frustration.
Fuelled by love.

When I got Reiji — my impossibly affectionate Brussels Griffon — I thought having a dog would simplify my life. It did the opposite. I was constantly switching between apps: one for walks, one to find dog-friendly spots, another for recipes, a Facebook group to find playdates. None of them talked to each other. None of them really understood what it means to live with a dog in the city.

So I built what I wished existed. Oui PAWD is Reiji's app as much as it's mine — designed for dog owners who want to do more with their dogs, not spend their evenings managing tabs.

We're community-first, and we're building this one city at a time — starting with the dog lovers who sign up today.
